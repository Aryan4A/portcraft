The Problem

Portfolio tools were broken

Every option was either too expensive (Webflow, Wix), too ugly (those old portfolio generators), too rigid (LinkedIn PDF exports), or required coding skills most students don't have yet.

I saw CS students sending Google Docs as their "portfolio" to companies. Developers with a 2012 WordPress site they haven't touched since. Talented people without any online presence at all.

So I built PortCraft. Fill a form, download an HTML file, upload it to GitHub Pages. Done. Free. Professional. Yours.

January 2025
The Idea
Frustrated with existing portfolio tools, I decided to build my own — a simple form that generates a real, downloadable portfolio in minutes.
March 2025
First Version Built
Basic form with live preview and one-click HTML download. Shared with friends — they loved it and asked for more features.
July 2025
Templates & Customization
Added Modern Dark, Neon Night, and Clean Light templates. Photo upload with base64 embedding. Accent color picker added.
January 2026

Complete multi-step builder with skill bars, project cards, education, experience sections and 3 layout styles. Launched publicly.

Growing & Improving
