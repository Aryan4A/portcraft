FAQ

Common questions

Is PortCraft really completely free? ›
Yes, 100% free. No free tier with limitations, no premium upgrade, no credit card. The entire product is free forever.
Does my data get saved to any server? ›
Absolutely not. Everything runs in your browser. Your name, photo, projects — none of it ever leaves your device. Close the tab and it's gone.
Can I edit the HTML after downloading? ›
Yes! The downloaded file is clean, readable HTML. Open it in VS Code and customize it however you want. It's your file, you own it completely.
Where can I host my portfolio for free? ›
GitHub Pages is the easiest — upload your file, enable Pages in settings, done. Netlify and Vercel also offer free hosting with drag-and-drop deploy.
Will my photo appear without external hosting? ›
Yes. Your photo gets embedded directly into the HTML file as base64. The portfolio is completely self-contained — no external image hosting needed.
How do I report a bug or request a feature? ›
Use the form on this page or email me directly at aryanpatil4554@gmail.com. You can also reach me on GitHub at github.com/Aryan4A.
